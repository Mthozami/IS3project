About Siza Library

Siza Library is a community-driven initiative that aims to bring knowledge and inspiration to people of all ages. Established in 2015, our library has grown into a trusted place for learning, connection, and creativity. Our library is a community-centered public library dedicated to serving students, researchers, and residents of the area. Located in the heart of our town, it offers a wide range of physical and digital resources to promote education, reading, and personal development.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower the community by providing free access to information, education, and cultural enrichment.

Our Vision

We envision a society where everyone has the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed through access to knowledge.

What Makes Us Unique?

Inclusive Environment

We welcome all members of the community regardless of age, background, or education level.

Innovative Learning

We host tech sessions, coding workshops, and digital literacy training for all ages.

Driven by Volunteers

Our library thrives thanks to passionate volunteers and community partnerships.

üìù How to Register as a Member

  • Complete a membership form in person or register online through our website.
  • Provide valid identification and proof of address.
  • Membership is free for students and local residents.
  • Once approved, you'll receive a library card for borrowing privileges.

üìö Rules for Borrowing Books

  • Members may borrow up to 1 book at a time.
  • Books are due back within 7 days of borrowing.
  • Renewals allowed once unless the item is reserved by another member.
  • Late returns incur a fine of R5 per day per book.
  • Lost or damaged items must be replaced or paid for in full.

üìñ Code of Conduct in the Library

  • Maintain silence to create a respectful environment for all users.
  • Eating and drinking are not permitted inside the library.
  • Handle all library materials with care.
  • Treat library staff and fellow members with respect.
  • Mobile phones must be on silent; calls in designated areas only.
